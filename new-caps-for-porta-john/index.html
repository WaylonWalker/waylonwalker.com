Today I dropped some new caps in the porta-john. I need to do a bunch of posts on zmk and my journey building the porta-john, I've been rocking it for at least two weeks now pretty solid and I am absolutely loving it. More on that later.

KLP-Lame-Keycaps

The model of the caps are KLP-Lame by braindefender , they are super rounded and smooth. They look really good on camera and feel good to the finders.

I printed these at home on my ender3 s1 pro with a black and purple silk filliment. I printed all 42 at once using a knock-and-sweep technique. They do have just a bit of a rough texture that you can feel on your figertips. I printed these on super quality .12mm layer height, while my last set was printed on draft quality .32mm layer height. They look crazy different in print quality with the lines of the new caps barely showing, the feel isn't nearly as drastic.
