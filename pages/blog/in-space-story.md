## Brainstorming


### Setting

* fire planet
* mining colony
* An abandoned drilling station

### Conflict

* An old drilling station has been taken over by pirates
* The old station was abandoned because it was too close to a pocket of explosive gas
* If they are able to start up the station they will be able to make unimaginable profits
* Doing so will cause an explosion that will take down nearby towns.

### Characters

* A young boy has discovered the old station has been taken over by pirates
* A Pirate Captain willing to risk it all for the profits
